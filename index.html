Experiments


Crank and Slider Mechanism

A virtual simulation of slider crank mechanism from which one can be able to determine the location of the piston of a reciprocating engine or compressor at any given crank angle, for a given crank and connecting rod length

Four bar Linkage

In this experiment, we can analyse different configurations of four-bar mechanism along with their configuration profiles. Also, mentioned about satisfaction of grashof's condition based on length of links and angular speed.
